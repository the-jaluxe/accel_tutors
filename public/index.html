Tutoring Services

Striving to provide the best and most effective tutoring services.

Satisfaction Guaranteed

If you are unhappy with any of my services for any reason, I will provide you with no charge for the session. No commitment is required so you can find a match that's right for you.

Subject Competency

I host tutoring services for both math and computer science for the grades of K-12 and college. Whether it's basic programming concepts or AP Computer Science, I will make sure that you succeed.

Competitive Rates

My tutoring services have rates that are competitive with other tutors and typically start at $50 per hour. This rate is flexible based on the difficulty of the class and services needed.

How To Start

Learn about the steps to arrange a tutoring session.

  • Contact Us

    Using the form below to get in contact with me and schedule your first session. Feel free to include available times, subjects needed, and the session length in your message.

  • Personal Contact

    I will personally reach out to confirm any details about the session. If you require any special requests for your session, please feel free to let me know and I will be sure to accommodate.

  • Tutoring Session

    Using online tutoring technologies, I will set up a session for you to join. I have experience using different methods of teaching that are most effective for the content you need help with.

  • Satisfaction Guaranteed

    Your first one hour session is free to guarantee that you are happy with my services. If you need additional help, I can arrange repeating sessions based on your schedule.

Meet The Founder



Jarrett Cornelison

Founder and Lead Tutor
Accel Tutors

Starting in high school, I took AP Computer Science A as a freshman and thoroughly enjoyed every part of it. With my natural talent in math and inspiration from this class, I would go on to take numerous honors and AP courses throughout high school including AP Calculus and AP statisics with a 4.32 overall GPA. Then, I went on to major in Computer Science at UC San Diego while recently graduating this past summer with a 3.64 GPA. I have experience tutoring throughout my academics and I enjoy every part of making difficult concepts in math and computer science very easy and engaging for all ages.
